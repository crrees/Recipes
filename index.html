Homemade Potato Waffles (Birds Eye-style)

Crispy outside, soft inside potato waffles using potato starch for that familiar texture.

Yield: 6 waffles
Prep: 15 min
Cook: 25 min
Total: 45 min
Cuisine: British
Category: Side

Ingredients

  • 500 g floury potatoes, peeled
  • 40 g potato starch
  • 1 egg white
  • 1/2 tsp fine salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp rapeseed oil, plus extra for brushing
  • Optional: splash of milk if mixture feels too dry

Method

  1. Boil and dry potatoes: Boil until very tender, drain, mash smooth, then spread to steam off 10 minutes.
  2. Make mixture: Mix mash with potato starch, salt and pepper. Add egg white and 1 tbsp oil. If crumbly, add a splash of milk.
  3. Cook: Preheat and oil waffle iron. Cook 6–8 minutes until golden. Optional oven finish 220 °C, 5 minutes.

Nutrition (per waffle)

  • Fat: 2.3 grams (sat 0.2 grams)

  • Fibre: 1.8 grams
  • Protein: 2.3 grams
